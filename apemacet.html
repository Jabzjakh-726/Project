AP EAMCET Eligibility Criteria 2023

The Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture, and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test (AP EAPCET) is formerly known as the Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture, and Medical Common Entrance Test (AP EAMCET). Every year, the official authorities hold the AP EAMCET as a single entrance test with the goal of providing admission chances to worthy students in various degrees provided in engineering, agriculture, pharmacy, and other professions.

On the official website, JNTU Anantapur published the AP EAMCET 2023 qualifying requirements as well as a booklet. The AP EAMCET eligibility criteria 2023 consists of the conditions and standards that candidates who wish to take the AP EAMCET test 2023 must meet.

Eligibility for APEMACET

    Nationality:

    Candidates should be Indian nationals or Persons of Indian Origin (PIO) / Overseas Citizen of India (OCI) cardholders.

    Domicile:

    Candidates should satisfy the local/non-local status requirements as laid down by the Andhra Pradesh / Telangana state educational authorities.

    Educational Qualification:

    • For Engineering Courses:

      Candidates should have passed or appeared in the final year of their 10+2 or equivalent examination with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry as main subjects.

    • For Medical and Agriculture Courses:

      Candidates should have passed or appeared in the final year of their 10+2 or equivalent examination with Biology, Physics, Chemistry, and/or Mathematics as main subjects.
