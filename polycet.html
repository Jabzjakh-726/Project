About Polycet

AP POLYCET normally called the Andhra Pradesh Polytechnic Common Entrance Test is a state-level entrance exam conducted by the Andhra Pradesh State Board of Technical Education and Training for candidates. For those who are seeking admission into various diploma and polytechnic courses - the exam is held offline for a duration of 2 hours across various centers of Andhra Pradesh. AP POLYCET 2023 Syllabus is based on topics from Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics from Class 9th and 10th of Andhra Pradesh Board.

AP POLYCET 2023 Eligibility Criteria

The applicant for AP POLYCET 2023 should possess all the basic eligibility criteria to appear for the entrance examination. The State Board of Technical Education and Training which is the conducting authority of the examination decides the eligibility criteria for AP POLYCET 2023.

  • The applicant must be a citizen of Indian nationality to appear for AP POLYCET 2023.
  • As per the Domicile: The applicant should belong to native Andhra Pradesh to appear in the examination.
  • There is no age limitation to appear for the AP POLYCET 2023 Exam.
  • The applicant must possess the basic educational Qualification that is clearing the 10th or equivalent exam from a recognized board with at least 35% marks and should have studied Math as a compulsory subject.

AP PolyCET 2023 Eligibility Criteria

The eligibility criteria of AP Polycet 2021 is listed below:

  1. Nationality:

    Only Indian students can apply for AP Polycet 2021.

  2. Domicile:

    Students who are applying for AP Polycet should be the residents of the state of Andra Pradesh.

  3. Education Qualification:

    The student should have cleared SSC examination or equivalent from a recognized university or board with aggregate marks of 35% in Mathematics and Science.

  4. Candidates Age:

    At present, there is no Age limit for the students applying for AP Polycet.

  5. State Eligibility:

    Applicants must belong to the state of Andhra Pradesh and they also should have a domicile of the state Andhra Pradesh.

  6. Mandatory Subjects:

    Students who belong to the National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) or AP Open School Society (APOSS) or Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) or Indian Council for Secondary Education (ICSE) or any other examination which is recognized by the Government of AP are eligible. Applicants must and should have Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics as their main subjects with at least 35% in each subjec

  7. Candidates Appearing:

    Students who are appearing for the SSC examination or who are waiting for the exam results can also apply for this examination. Students who have a compartment in their SSC examination, are also eligible to apply for the examination. But, they have to clear their compartment before the admission process begins.

AP POLYCET Syllabus 2023

The AP POLYCET exam mainly consists of three subjects- Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics. The questions are asked on the basis of Class 9 and Class 10 syllabi. It is therefore important for the students to have a deep understanding of three subjects to score better marks.

Given below are the subject-wise important topics for AP POLYCET Exam 2023:

  1. Physics

    1. Units and dimensions Work, Power, and Energy.
    2. Elements of vectors Heat and Thermodynamics
    3. Kinematics and Friction
    4. Simple harmonic motion and acoustics
    5. Reflection of Light Refraction of Light at Plane Surface
    6. Refraction of Light at Curved Surface Modern Physics

  2. Chemistry

    1. Atomic Structure
    2. Chemical Bonding
    3. Water Technology
    4. Solutions Fuels
    5. Electro-Chemistry
    6. Acids and Bases
    7. Corrosion Polymers
    8. Environmental chemistry

  3. Maths

    1. Number System
    2. Real Numbers
    3. Linear Equations
    4. Polynomials
    5. Algebra
    6. Quadratic Equations
    7. Trigonometry
    8. Progressions
    9. Sets
    10. Geometry
    11. Surface Areas & Volume
    12. Mensuration
