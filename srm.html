Eligiblity Criteria

  • Nationality

    • Citizens of India will be able to apply and appear for SRMJEEE 2023. Non-Resident Indian (NRI), PIO, or OCI cardholders can also apply for SRMJEEE. Further, there will also be an option to apply to the International student category, if candidates do not wish to appear for the entrance examination.

  • Age Criteria-

    • Candidate should complete 16 years of age on or before July 31, 2023.

  • Year of Passing 10+2 (or equivalent exam)-

    • Candidates who will be appearing for the qualifying examination in 2023 will also be considered eligible.

  • The Candidate must have an aggregate score of 60% or equivalent grade points in Grade 10 Examination.
  • The Candidate must have an aggregate score of 60% or equivalent grade points in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics in Grade 12 Examination in any of the Indian Boards recognised by the Board of Higher Secondary Education, Delhi.
  • Students appearing for the Grade 12 Boards Examination in the current academic year are also eligible to apply for the exam.
