RMO, or Regional Mathematical Olympiad, is the regional level of the International Olympiad for Mathematics, in India. It is held across 25 regions throughout India. The Homi Bhabha Center of Science Education (HBCSE) conducts the exam to encourage young minds in the field of Mathematics. They will eventually represent India in the International Mathematical Olympiad program.


For RMO Eligibility, an applicant has to clear the Pre RMO Exams at the first level of the International Mathematics Olympiad. From an All India regional level test to a national level, RMO is the stepping stone which leads to international glory for the participants from our country.


Eligblity

Given below are the major Pre RMO eligibility 2022 as set by the National Board for Higher Mathematics (NBHM) which is in charge of the mathematical olympiad activity:

  • Candidates must be born on or after August 1, 2001, to be eligible to apply for Pre RMO 2022.
  • Candidates studying in Class 8, 9, 10, 11, and 12 are eligible to appear in the Pre RMO exam.
  • Only Indian citizens will be allowed to write the Pre RMO examination. Candidates who are the holders of Overseas Citizenship of India or OCI cards can also take the exam. However, there is one condition, they will not be eligible for selection in the International Mathematical Olympiad.

Students must ensure that they meet the eligibility norms set by the authorities. If they are found to be disobeying the eligibility rules, their candidature can be cancelled at any stage. No exception, whatsoever, will be granted to candidates. Once you are aware that you satisfy the eligibility requirements, you can proceed with filling up the registration form. Candidates are required to pay a registration fee of Rs 230. However, Kendriya Vidyalaya (KV) and Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV) are supposed to pay a reduced fee of 125 and 100 respectively.


Candidates who are interested in applying for Mathematics Olympiad programmes in India must note that the authorities are strict regarding all the policies and norms. Pre RMO paves the way for your dreams of becoming a national Mathematical Olympiad champion. Hence, aspirants must begin the exam preparation process as early as possible. Performing well in Pre RMO is crucial to qualify for Regional Mathematical Olympiad (RMO).
