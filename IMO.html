What is IMO Olympaid

The International Math Olympiad (IMO) is a prestigious mathematical competition conducted for school kids at the international level. It is organised annually by the SOF - Science Olympiad Foundation in which kids from around the world compete in solving challenging mathematical problems. The aim of the IMO Olympiad is to identify and encourage students who have a talent and aptitude for mathematics, and to provide them with a platform to showcase their skills.

IMO Eligibility Criteria 2023

The eligibility criteria for IMO Maths Olympiad 2023 in India are as follows:

  • The candidate should be studying in class 1 to 12.
  • The candidate should be studying in a school that is affiliated with an educational board recognized by the government.
  • The candidate must be registered by his/her school for SOF.

There are no other specific IMO 2023 Eligibility Criteria. However, it is important to note that the exam is meant for students who have an aptitude and interest in mathematics, and is not mandatory for all students.

Syllabus

Level 1:

  • The syllabus for grades 1 to 4 includes topics such as Numbers, Basic Geometrical Shapes, Money, Measurement of Length, Weight, Capacity, Time and Temperature, Geometrical Shapes, Patterns, Odd One Out, and Serie
  • The syllabus for grades 5 to 12 includes topics such as Number\ System, Algebra, Geometry, Mensuration, Data Handling, Logical Reasoning, Mathematical Reasoning, and Everyday Mathematics.

Level 2:

  • The syllabus for grades 5 to 12 includes topics such as Geometry, Number Theory, Algebra, Combinatorics, and Probability.
  • The questions are designed to test the students' creativity, ingenuity, and ability to apply mathematical principles to real-world problems.

It is important for students to have a strong foundation in mathematics and a deep understanding of the topics covered in the syllabus. They should practise solving problems from past papers and participate in training camps and workshops to improve their problem-solving skills.
