TS EAMCET Eligibility Criteria 2023

For admission to engineering, agriculture, and medical degrees, the Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test, or TS EAMCET 2023, will be conducted. The notification will be available soon on the official website at eamcet.tsche.ac.in. regarding the TS EAMCET exam. Candidates seeking admission to several UG programmes in the Engineering (E) and Agriculture and Medical (AM) course will have to take the exams. Passing the exam will allow candidates to enrol in E and AM courses of their choice at private and public colleges around the state.

It should be noted that if a candidate does not meet the minimum TS EAMCET 2023 eligibility standards, they will not be considered for final admission. We have included all the information that candidates need to know about the TS EAMCET 2023 eligibility criteria in this article.

Eligibility for TS EMACET

    Nationality:

    Candidates should be Indian nationals or Persons of Indian Origin (PIO) / Overseas Citizen of India (OCI) cardholders.

    Domicile:

    Candidates should satisfy the local/non-local status requirements as laid down by the Telangana state educational authorities.

    Educational Qualification:

    • For Engineering Courses:

      Candidates should have passed or appeared in the final year of their 10+2 or equivalent examination with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry as main subjects.

    • For Medical and Agriculture Courses:

      Candidates should have passed or appeared in the final year of their 10+2 or equivalent examination with Biology, Physics, Chemistry, and/or Mathematics as main subjects.
